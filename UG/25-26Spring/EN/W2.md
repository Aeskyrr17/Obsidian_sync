## Paragraph 1 — Introduction

**目标：90–120 words**

### 这一段必须做到

- very brief introduction of topic
- 明确 thesis
- 告诉读者你这篇 blog 要讨论什么

### 内容顺序

1. Makerspaces are increasingly valued in STEM/engineering education
2. They are often praised for creativity and hands-on learning
3. But their value should not be taken for granted
4. Thesis: they matter most when participation is inclusive and equitable

### 这一段不能做的事

- 不要写太多定义
- 不要开始详细 summary source
- 不要太像 W1 那种 “This blog summarizes and evaluates…” 的模板句

---

## Paragraph 2 — Website source summary

**目标：120–150 words**

### 这一段的功能

- summary source 1
- 但不要只是复述
- 段尾必须开始连接你的 thesis

### 应该写什么

- EdSurge article argues that an inclusive makerspace must be built deliberately
- A makerspace is not inclusive simply because it has tools
- Educators need to think about who feels welcome and who may still be excluded

### 这一段最后必须加一句你的 voice

例如：  
**This article is useful because it shifts attention from equipment to participation, suggesting that access alone does not create inclusion.**

这句就是“你的 reflection”，不是 study critique。

---

## Paragraph 3 — Journal source summary

**目标：150–190 words**

### 这一段的功能

- summary source 2
- 说明 research focus 和重要 findings
- 同时往 synthesis 靠

### 应该写什么

- Roldan et al. examine women’s participation in university makerspaces
- The study shows that belonging depends on social and structural factors, not just physical access
- Mention the most relevant mechanisms:
    - project assessment
    - member assessment
    - signals of approachability
    - structured help-seeking

### 这一段最后也要有你的 voice

例如：  
**Compared with the website article, this study explains more concretely why some students may still feel excluded even in spaces that appear open and innovative.**

这句很重要，因为它已经不是 summary，而是在比较 source purpose。

---

## Paragraph 4 — Synthesis

**目标：180–220 words**  
**这是全篇最重要的一段。**

### 这一段必须做到

- summarize and synthesize major findings from the two sources
- 有 clear thesis development
- 有 your own insight

### 这一段建议写法

你可以按这个顺序：

#### 先写共同点

两篇都不同意“makerspace 天然包容”这种想法。

#### 再写区别

- EdSurge 是 public-facing, practical, broad
- Roldan et al. 是 research-based, specific, systematic

#### 然后写你的 synthesis

例如：

**Taken together, the two sources suggest that the success of a makerspace does not come from tools alone. Instead, it depends on whether students are supported socially as well as physically.**

#### 再加你的 reflection

例如：  
**This is especially important in engineering, where students who already feel less confident or less represented may interpret an unfamiliar space as a sign that they do not belong.**

这一句就是老师想看到的“your reflection on those ideas”。

---

## Paragraph 5 — Critique of sources and source selection

**目标：150–180 words**

这段是你 W1 最容易写偏的地方，所以这次要分清：

### 这段不是只写 study limitations

而是要同时写两层 critique：

#### A. critique of source selection

- Why is the website useful?
- Why is the journal useful?
- Why do they work well together?

#### B. critique of the ideas / limitations

- website article is accessible and suitable for public readers, but less systematic
- journal article is more credible and detailed, but narrower in population and context
- neither source fully answers whether inclusive design works the same way across all disciplines or institutions

### 这一段最重要的一句

你必须写出：  
**what these sources still do not fully explain**

例如：  
**Together, the two sources show why inclusion matters, but they say less about how universities can measure whether their makerspaces are truly becoming more equitable over time.**

这就是老师要的“reflection on ideas”，不是单纯说 sample size 小。

---

## Paragraph 6 — Conclusion

**目标：70–90 words**

### 这一段功能

- 重申 thesis
- 收束 synthesis
- 可以 very short advice，但不要太展开

### 推荐方向

- makerspaces should be understood as learning communities, not just technical spaces
- inclusion is central, not secondary
- therefore the educational value of makerspaces depends on intentional design

# 这个版本为什么比你 W1 更符合要求

## 你 W1 更像这样：

- intro
- long summary
- study strengths
- study weaknesses
- conclusion

这个结构的问题是：  
**source 很多，你自己很少**

## 你 W2 应该变成这样：

- intro with thesis
- source 1 summary with brief reflection
- source 2 summary with brief reflection
- synthesis with your insight
- critique of source selection + critique of ideas
- conclusion

这个结构里：  
**每一段都有一点“你的声音”**

这才更符合：

- clear thesis
- synthesis
- critique
- coherence

# How to Read a Paper

## Three-Pass Reading
### Pass 1: Bird's-eye View
- Title
- Abstract
- Section headings
- Figures and tables
- Conclusion
- Decide whether to continue

### Pass 2: Understand the Logic
- Problem
- Method
- Inputs and outputs
- Experiments
- Limitations

### Pass 3: Deep Reading
- Derivations
- Code
- Reproduction
- Critical evaluation
